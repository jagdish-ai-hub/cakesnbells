Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 6, 2026

Welcome to Cakes N Bells. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our application and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in strict data minimization. We do not actively collect, store, sell, or rent any of your personal data or usage analytics.

The only information utilized by our application is the data strictly necessary for order processing (such as your name, phone number, and delivery details provided voluntarily during checkout). This information is processed transparently to generate your order.

2. Data Storage and Retention

Your order details are never stored on our remote servers. Any information you provide during the order creation process is used momentarily to format your order message. We do not maintain any backend databases of user personal information. Temporary preferences (such as your wishlist) are stored locally on your own device and never transmitted to us.

3. Use of Data

Any data provided during an order is used exclusively to:

  • Process and fulfill your specific purchase efficiently.
  • Enable direct communication with you regarding the status of your order via your chosen messaging app (e.g., WhatsApp).

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party tracking metrics, analytics software, or targeted advertising services that profile or collect personal information about you.

5. Data Security

The security of your data is paramount. Because all order processing hands off directly to secure messaging protocols and we do not store your data centrally, the risk of data breaches involving your personal information is effectively eliminated.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your order information, please reach out directly through our app's contact methods.
